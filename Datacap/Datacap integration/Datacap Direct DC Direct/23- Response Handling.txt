Two copies must be printed; one for presentation to the cardholder (a separate receipt from the usual receipt) and one for retention by the merchant. Line# elements are included in responses that can be used to print the receipt document(s). Before printing receipt lines the POS system should print the merchant name, address and phone number as separate lines at the top of the receipt. After printing these lines the POS must add the phrase “Cardholder Copy” as the last line of the first receipt copy and the phrase “Merchant Copy” as the last line of the second receipt copy.
